About Me

An Abbreviated Biography

I was born in Brownwood, Texas. At the time my father was in the United States Air force. Not long after I was born my family moved to Kadena Air Force Base in Okinawa, Japan.

We lived in Okinawa for a few years and then my father was transferred to Fort Meade, Maryland. We remained in Maryland until my father got out of the military.

After my father left the military, we moved around quite a bit with his job to different cities and States. It wasn’t until the mid-1970’s that our family finally settled in Waco, Texas. Where my family has since remained. I went to High School and College in the Waco area. I later moved to Houston, Texas for a job. Eventually, I returned to Waco, Texas to be near my family.

I like to travel and meet new people. I have been to many places, but I think my favorite place to visit is Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Every time I go there, I still find so much in the Vancouver area that I have yet to see and explore.
