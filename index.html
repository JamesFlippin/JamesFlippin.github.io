About Me

An Abbreviated Biography

I was born in Brownwood, Texas. At the time my father was in the United States Air force. Not long after I was born the family soon moved to Fort Meade, Maryland where we lived for a while. Eventually, my father (and family) was transferred to Kadena Air Force Base in Okinawa, Japan.

We lived in Okinawa for a few years and then my father was again transferred back to Fort Meade, Maryland. We remained in Maryland until my father got out of the military.

After my father left the military, we moved around quite a bit with his job to different cities and States. It wasn’t until the mid-1970’s that our family finally settled in Waco, Texas. Where my family has since remained. I went to High School and College in Waco. But eventually moved to Houston, Texas for a while for a job. Eventually, I returned to Waco, Texas to be near my family.
